About Me

My name is Lynda Slayen and I live in Bethesda Maryland with my husband Gary. I have always loved art but I first started working with fused glass in Glen Echo Park in 2004. Since then I have created thousands of pieces, which I have sold at various local art events.
I also love teaching others the art of fused glass and I frequently teach classes for children of all ages!
